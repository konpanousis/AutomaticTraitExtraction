Shrub or tree, occasionally up to 30.0 m high, trunk 8.0-20.0, rarely up to 100.0 cm diam, bark gray.
Branchlets slender, tips glabrous, older parts early brownish-corticate and set with numerous small pale lenticels.
Sepals 6-9, imbricate, strongly unequal, thin, concave, broadly elliptic to ovate or obovate, greenish-whitish, scented, glabrous, reflexed, 4.0-6.0 mm long, 2.0-3.0 mm broad.
Flowering color: white.
Definition: whole plant arborescent.
Capsule subglobose, fleshy, green to purple-red, glossy, finally opening with 3 or 4 valves which are carmine inside, 3.0-4.0 cm diam, on stoutish peduncle, seeds rather numerous, mutually ovoid-angled, 3.0-4.0 mm long, testa brownish-blackish, tubercled, aril membranous, orange.
In prep.
Stamens 15-24, filaments densely to laxly, sometimes hardly pubescent, 3.0-4.0 mm long, anthers subsagittate, 1.0-1.5 mm long.
Shrub, Small tree, Tree.
Inflorescences consisting of 3-6-flowered clusters or much abbreviated corymbs borne on top of very short pseudobranches, bracts ovate-subulate, membranous, ca 2.0 mm long, pedicels slender, glabrous, 0.5-1.5 cm long.
Disk-lobes fixed within the row of stamens, tomentose, 1.0-1.5 mm long.
Ovary subglobose, laxly pubescent or glabrous, tapering to the slender style , stigma capitate.
