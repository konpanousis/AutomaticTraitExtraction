Leaves opposite or subopposite, blades 1.5- 4.5 x 0.5-2.5 cm, elliptic to elliptic-ovate, chartaceous, glabrous, upper surface usually with hispidulous, prominulous midvein, lower surface obscurely punctate, the apex obtuse to shortly acuminate, the acumen blunt, the base obtuse, the margins entire, petioles 2-3 mm long, hispidulous.
Growth Habit - Tree, Shrub.
Flowers fragrant, in short axillary racemes , axis ca.
General Information Shrub 2-5 m tall, bark light gray, smooth, twigs hispidulous, becoming glabrous.
E. Boom met tot 20 cm.
Hypanthium light green, cup-shaped, 0.7-1 mm long, glabrous, the sepals ovate, ciliate, in unequal pairs, 0.5-1 mm long, petals white, obovate, concave, reflexed, 3-3.5 mm long, disk ca.
The flowers are solitary or in small clusters and produce berry fruits.
The aromatic, glossy leaves are borne oppositely along the stems and are usually simple with entire margins.
