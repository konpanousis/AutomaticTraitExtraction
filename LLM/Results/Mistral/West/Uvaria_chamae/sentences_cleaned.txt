Definition: whole plant arborescent.
Scandent shrub or small tree to 10 ft.high.
The flowers are borne singly, in pairs, or in small clusters.
It is orange on the outside an orangeish yellow flesh interior.
The fruit forms in a habit similar to other Annonaceae, such as Asimina triloba.
There are six petals in two whorls and many stamens.
Inconspicuous yellow flowers born in bunches Fruit: A fruit that forms in bunches similar to bananas.
Fruits rusty-brown.
A small nondescript Sapotaceae flower that is born on the trunks and mature branches of the tree.
