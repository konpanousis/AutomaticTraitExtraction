An attractive shrub with attractive glossy green foliage.
Tree, shrub over 2 m.
Flowers bright yellow, very short-lived, normally appearing before or with the young leaves.
Leaves oblanceolate to oblong or elliptic, margins rather bluntly serrulate, sometimes appearing scalloped, net-veining conspicuous above.
This display is followed by shiny black fruit suspended below bright red sepals.
Fruits ovoid, attached at the base, black when ripe, the enlarged, persistent sepals turning cherry to brick red.
