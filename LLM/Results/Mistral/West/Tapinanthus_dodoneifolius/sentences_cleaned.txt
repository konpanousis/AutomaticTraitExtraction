Well-branched shrub with spreading and pendent branches to 1.5 m. long, glabrous, branchlets slightly 3-angular or flattened, soon terete.
Berry orange-red to red, obovoid-ellipsoid, 1-1.5 mm.
Flowers white, golden yellow, deep rose or red.
Style dark red, 5-winged especially on the thickened part opposite vents in bud, neck 5-6 mm.
Leaves opposite to alternate or ternate, petiole 3-10 mm.
Leaves opposite, alternate or ternate.
Filaments red, involute, stout, flattened, tooth 1 mm.
Calyx tubular, 4-6 mm.
