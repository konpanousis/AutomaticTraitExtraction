The leaflets have golden glands on the undersurface which can be seen with a hand lens.
Definitie: An autotroph which is capable of transforming light into chemical energy.
Our species, Markhamia tomentosa, is a medium sized tree, often with a fluted trunk and exfoliating bark.
The two stipules are narrow and pointed and pressed against the bud in the axil of each leaf.
Fruits/seeds: long narrow flat capsule, often twisted and opening on the tree when ripe, containing many rectangular, pale, flat, winged seeds.

Type of seed: recalcitrant.
