Leaves, alternate and imparipinnate, up to 23 cm long, with 2-3 leaflets per pinnae, leaf-blade is narrowly ovate in outline, 5-13 cm long and 2.5-6 cm wide, leaflets have a rough and waxy adaxial surface.
Fruits are ellipsoid in shape, grows in raceme like bundles of between 3-25, purple to blackish color when ripe.
Scarification on the distal end led to delayed radicle emergence through the produced hole in c. 40% of the seeds.
The species is capable of growing up to 15 m high with a rather short trunk and dense crown, it has a grey and smooth bark with a reddish and white fibrous slash.
